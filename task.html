Frequent mistakes

Students often get confused while buying their first 'machine-friends'. Many advertisements from different companies and stores make people to forget about their aims. In most cases buyers overpay for unnecessary characteristics of the laptop. You have to define for what else you are gonna use it clearly.

For instance, I have many friends who bought laptop for gamers, while they didn't use to play computer games. Note, those types are expensive for special graphics cards. There are many similar cases.

Below are listed only standard criteria.

Display

Because you will be using your computer for several hours every day, the quality of the display plays an important role. Generally, most cheap laptops provide screen only with 1366 x 768px. That is suitable for people who do not use their computers a lot. Working in front of screen with around 1366 x 768px for long time make your eyes to get tired quickly.

Search for a Full-HD screen. Resolution must be high. This may cost quitely more expensive, but remember about your eyes.

Also, make sure that the display has comfortable viewing angles.

CPU

CPU is a brain of computer, it is responsible for performance of commands sent by you. CPU stands for Central Processing Unit. The time rate is measured by Hz. The higher the rate, the more actions it can perform at exact amount of time. That is, you should pay attention at the speed of the processor (CPU).

Intel's processors got popular nowadays, which are considered to be the most efficient processors. So you will see only them at most. There are several spread versions, even if they are old. Do not buy Intel i3. It is much slower than Intel i5 and i7. Actually there isn't big difference between Intel i5 and i7, so if there is no need to overpay for later version of these 2.

Also, pay attention on the amount of cores. The more they are, the faster your computer calculates.

Here is 1 example of CPU notion in stores. Intel Core i5-7300HQ.
7 is for generation. 7th and higher generation are good.
300 is for measurement of performance, actually this depends on other CPU parameters.
H is important for 3D modeling. Find more about it, if you plan to learn modeling deeply during study.
Q means that the processor contains 4 cores. This is good for programmers. Note: when you see U or Y instead, the processor has only 2 cores or it means that the performance level is low. This is unsatisfactory for software engineering.

RAM

For projects of the hardness at the average level the minimum amount of space in RAM is 16GB. But, because you only study, your laptop's RAM can contain lower. Note: 4GB is needed at least.

RAM stands for Random-Access-Memory. This is like computer's work area for executing a program. The reason why RAM's space is important is that, bofore the program is executed in CPU, it goes to computer's memory. During hard programs' executions small amount of memory space in working device gives out errors.

Note: modern programs consume a lot of space. 8GB and 16GB are better choices. But for the study of 3-4 years 4GB is also acceptable.

Internal storage

The main criterion that must be mentioned is the type of hard disc. Choose SSD, it is faster than the standard hard disc. Presence of SSD is one more factor for price to be higher. With SSD every operation will be executed faster: programs' compilation time, projects' loading, applications' launching, etc.

The minimum capacity of SSD must be 256GB. Just imagine your phone having very low storage capacity. It is annoying sometimes. Many warnings come from the system that the space is not enough after certain time of use. The same thing eith computer. If you save your money for lower space of SSD, you will meet such problems very soon. Of course, this doesn't affect on programs execution. But your data must be back-upped in correct way.

Additional properties

As it was mentioned earlier, define your aims before you choose your laptop. There maybe more criteria for your laptop accordimg to your interests. During the study time, you can use your computer for computer games, video editing, photoshop, and all other hobbies.

Here are some additional parameters of computers, that are not very important as the previous ones, but also play a big role in some usage.

Graphics card
This is very necessary for mobilography, 3D modeling, computer games. There are 2 types of video cards: built-in and discrete. Only discrete video cards are appropriate for this use.
nVidia is strongly resommended. This company's cards are usually named as GeForce. Here is the example of nVidia's video cards notion: GeForce GTX1050. The last 2 digits show the level of performance. The first ones show the generation. The later generations are more suitable.

Compactness
Because you are a student, you always carry your laptop with yourself. Do not buy too heavy laptops. It makes you tired sometimes.

Keyboard
Try to type on the laptop before the purchase. The keyboard must be suitable for your work. Do not buy laptops with too large or too small keyboards.
